About

Welcome to Guardian Angel Care and Support. We are a team of kind, trained, and reliable caregivers. Our main goal is to help people who need extra support to live safely and comfortably at home.We know that every person is unique. That is why we listen closely to your needs and create a care plan just for you. Whether you need help with daily chores, meals, or just need a friendly companion, we are here to support you. We treat every client like a member of our own family. With us, you are never alone.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide safe, loving, and high-quality care to everyone we serve. We work hard every day to help people stay independent, happy, and comfortable in their own homes.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to be the most trusted care team in our community. We want to build a world where families feel at peace, and where every individual feels safe, respected, and loved.

  • Kindness: We treat everyone with a warm heart and a gentle smile.

    Respect: We value your choices, your privacy, and your home.

  • Trust: We are always honest, dependable, and safe.

    Family: We care for your loved ones just like we care for our own.

Services

Health Services

Providing medical care, check-ups, and physical health support to help people stay well.

Psychosocial Support

Offering mental health support, emotional care, and counseling for people and families dealing with stress or difficult times.

Advocacy

Speaking up for people's rights and making sure that everyone in the community gets fair treatment and the help they deserve.

Awareness Education

Teaching the community about healthy living, safety, and important wellness choices through classes or information sessions.
